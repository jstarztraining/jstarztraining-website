# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Halifax & Nova Scotia families, youth players (ages 6 → U18) and adult private clients, and parents researching private/small-group soccer and goalkeeper training. They arrive on phones, often mid-decision, comparing JStarz against templated Wix/Shopify-theme competitors. Their job-to-be-done: quickly trust that this is a serious, welcoming, high-touch program and get to the right Shopify product to book.

## Product Purpose
The public marketing site for JStarz Private Soccer Training (owner Jordan Ellis). It tells the JStarz story, showcases programs/coaches/schedule/testimonials/FAQ, and funnels visitors OUT to Shopify for checkout — the site never handles money. Content is editable via an admin dashboard backed by the same DB (ISR). Success = a premium, fast, accessible site that out-classes every local competitor and converts visits into bookings.

## Brand Personality
Athletic, warm, community-first. Confident without ego. Voice: "No ego. No politics. Just soccer." Energetic and dynamic in motion, but grounded and human — "less like training, more like a family." Emotional goals: confidence, belonging, momentum.

## Anti-references
- Template/Wix/Shopify-theme sites (the thing we must beat).
- Generic SaaS navy-and-gold corporate look — we share the palette only because it's the *existing* JStarz crest brand; execution must feel athletic and alive, not fintech-corporate.
- Editorial-magazine aesthetics (display-serif + drop caps) — wrong register for a sports brand.
- AI-slop tells: tiny tracked uppercase eyebrow above every section, identical icon-card grids, gradient text, glassmorphism-by-default.

## Design Principles
- **Earn the premium.** Every fold should look intentionally art-directed, not assembled — spacing, type, and motion do the talking.
- **Motion is a personality, not decoration.** One consistent dynamic motion language top-to-bottom (scroll-reveal, staggered cards, count-up, subtle parallax), always GPU-light and `prefers-reduced-motion`-aware.
- **Community over corporate.** Warmth and people first; the brand is a family, not an institution.
- **Speed and SEO are non-negotiable.** Never trade Core Web Vitals or semantics for flourish.
- **Funnel clarity.** Every program path leads cleanly to Shopify; the site informs and routes, it never transacts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast; one H1 per page; semantic landmarks; visible focus states; full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for every animation; descriptive alt text. Mobile-first.
